About Us

Are you in search of the top restaurant in Ottawa to enjoy authentic Canadian and Mediterranean meals with friends and family? Then come and check out Big H Restaurant! Our inviting restaurant is designed to appeal to our guests searching for a taste of authentic Mediterranean food, while enjoying a new dine-in experience with work colleagues or family.

At Big H Restaurant, we specialize in preparing fresh, hot meals that are made just the way you like it! Our menu features a full array of top-quality breakfast options, along with a great variety of sandwiches, burgers, shawarma, and leafy salads. Pick your taste and explore our menu today!

Whether you are ordering breakfast for the office or joining us at our location on 3210 Swansea Crescent, we have something to satisfy all your cravings! Start your day off with one of our classic breakfast options like the House Breakfast. One order is served with three eggs and your choice of ham, four strips of bacon, and three sausages. If you are craving something larger, choose from our Breakfast For The Big Appetite menu. Popular choices include the 22 22, Trucker Special, and the Hungry Man.

Are you joining us for lunch? Are you craving shawarma? Take a bite into our signature Chicken Shawarma Platter. We also have a delicious BBQ Combo Platter that is served with three skewers of beef, chicken, and kafta. We guarantee these meals will hit the spot!

We invite you to come and check out Big H Restaurant for great food, good company, and great service! We also offer online ordering courtesy of SkipTheDishes. Explore our menu and treat your cravings with an order of your favourite meal from Big H Restaurant!
